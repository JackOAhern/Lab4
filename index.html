Jack Ahern is a Data Science and Design student at the University of Colorado Boulder. He is interested in pursuing a career in UX Design.

UX Design

User experience design (UX Design) is the process of creating evidence-based, interaction designs between human users and products/websites. Design decisions in UX design are driven by research, data analysis, and test results rather than aesthetic preferences and opinions

Data Science

Data science is a field that uses scientific methods, processes, algorithms and systems to extract knowledge and insights from noisy, structured and unstructured data, and apply knowledge and actionable insights from data across a broad range of application domains.

Importance of Accesible Design

Accesible design can benefit everyone who interacts with your material and can save you time and effort in the future while benefiting the largest number of people in the present. It is important to think about how everyone will be able to interact with your designs and allow users with disabilities to access all relevant information.
